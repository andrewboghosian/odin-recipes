Pesto Chicken Sandwich

Description

Introducing our mouthwatering Pesto Chicken Sandwich, a delectable creation that combines the rich flavors of tender grilled chicken and aromatic pesto sauce. This sandwich is a harmonious blend of savory and fresh ingredients, perfect for those seeking a burst of flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Ciabatta Rolls
  • Pesto
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Arugula

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken breast
  2. Brush olive oil on bread and pesto
  3. Put chicken then cheese on top of chicken, then rest of ingredients
