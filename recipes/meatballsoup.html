Meatball Soup

DESCRIPTION

This recipe has been handed down for generations. We love this recipe.

We hope you enjoy it as well.

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts water
  • 20 small meatballs
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • Macaroni

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Make the meatballs
  3. Make the meatball soup
