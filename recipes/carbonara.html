Carbonara Recipe

Description

Indulge in the creamy richness of a traditional Italian favorite with this classic Carbonara recipe. This delectable dish combines perfectly cooked pasta with a velvety sauce, crispy pancetta, and a hint of Parmesan cheese. It's a symphony of flavors that will leave your taste buds longing for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup parmesaen cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Cook the bacon
  3. Add oil and garlic, cook until fragrant
  4. Add eggs and bacon
